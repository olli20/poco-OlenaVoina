FARMER, GOAT, CABBAGE, WOLF
A farmer needs to cross a river with a wolf, a goat, and a cabbage. 
He can only take one item with him at a time.
If left together, the wolf will eat the goat, and the goat will eat the cabbage. 
How can the farmer get all three items across the river safely?
